# Project Limitations

## Current Limitations

### Hardware Constraints

Experiments were conducted on a CPU-only environment with approximately 8 GB RAM.

Large-scale benchmark evaluation and inference using larger LLMs may require GPU resources.

### Benchmark Coverage

Current implementation focuses primarily on GSM8K reasoning evaluation.

Additional benchmarks such as TruthfulQA and HellaSwag are planned for future integration.

### Model Coverage

Current experiments are limited to prototype evaluation workflows.

Comprehensive evaluation across Gemma, Llama, and Mistral models remains future work.

### Dataset Scale

Only a small benchmark subset was used during development and testing.

Full-scale benchmark execution is planned in future versions.
